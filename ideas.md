# Дизайн Идеи — Mountain Ultra-Lux Pamporovo

## Подход 1: Cinematic Noir Alpine
<response>
<text>
**Design Movement:** Cinematic Noir Minimalism × Swiss Grid
**Core Principles:**
- Rich black (#0A0A0A) as the dominant canvas — darkness creates luxury
- Muted Gold (#C5A059) used only as a whisper — thin lines, hover states, small accents
- Every element earns its place through negative space and restraint
- Typography as architecture — Cinzel for monumental headings, Satoshi for body

**Color Philosophy:** The palette mirrors a Pamporovo night: deep black sky, golden firelight, white snow. Black dominates (90%), gold punctuates (5%), white breathes (5%). This creates a sense of exclusivity — like a private members club.

**Layout Paradigm:** Asymmetric editorial layout. Sections break the grid intentionally. Text blocks float left while images bleed to the right edge. Numbers are oversized and ghost-transparent. Horizontal scroll for the concierge section.

**Signature Elements:**
- Ultra-thin gold horizontal rule lines between sections
- Oversized ghost numerals (01–07) as background texture
- Letter-spaced uppercase labels in gold for section identifiers

**Interaction Philosophy:** Everything responds with weight and inertia. Magnetic cursor expands on hover. Scroll is smooth and cinematic. Nothing snaps — everything glides.

**Animation:** GSAP + ScrollTrigger. Hero text: each letter slides up from below with stagger. Parallax on all background images. Mask-reveal on image sections. WebGL liquid distortion on image transitions in the apartment collection.

**Typography System:**
- Display: Cinzel (400, 700) — monumental, classical, timeless
- Body: Satoshi (300, 400) — clean, modern, geometric
- Labels: Cinzel letter-spaced uppercase
</text>

## Подход 2: Brutalist Luxury
<response>
<text>
**Design Movement:** Brutalist Luxury × Architectural Minimalism
**Core Principles:**
- Raw contrast: pure white sections slam against pitch black
- Typography as the primary visual element — enormous, bold, structural
- Deliberate tension between harshness and warmth
- Grid breaks are intentional acts of design

**Color Philosophy:** Binary contrast with gold as the only warmth. White (#FFFFFF) and Black (#0A0A0A) create stark drama. Gold (#C5A059) appears only in micro-details — a single line, a hover state.

**Layout Paradigm:** Brutalist grid with intentional misalignment. Oversized type bleeds off-screen. Images are cropped aggressively. Sections have unexpected proportions.

**Signature Elements:**
- Giant cropped typography bleeding off edges
- Stark black/white section alternation
- Thin gold rule lines as the only decorative element

**Interaction Philosophy:** Bold, decisive interactions. No gentle fades — elements snap into place with authority. Cursor is a sharp crosshair that transforms.

**Animation:** Fast, decisive transitions. Text slams in. Images reveal with hard wipes. Scroll-jacking for dramatic effect.

**Typography System:**
- Display: Monument Grotesk (800) — architectural, bold, modern
- Body: Inter (300) — clean contrast to the heavy display
</text>

## Подход 3: Dark Cinematic Minimalist (ИЗБРАН)
<response>
<text>
**Design Movement:** Cinematic Minimalism × Swiss Design Precision
**Core Principles:**
- Darkness as luxury: #0A0A0A base creates depth and exclusivity
- Restraint in gold: #C5A059 appears only at key moments — like candlelight
- Asymmetric Swiss grid: content breathes, never centered generically
- Cinematic pacing: every scroll reveals something new, like a film cut

**Color Philosophy:** The palette is inspired by a luxury alpine evening — deep matte black of the night sky, the warm gold of a fireplace, the crisp off-white of fresh snow. This creates a sensory experience before the user even reads a word.

**Layout Paradigm:** Left-anchored editorial layout with deliberate asymmetry. Hero text sits bottom-left. Section numbers float as ghost elements. Images bleed to screen edges. The apartment collection uses a full-screen scroll-jacked experience.

**Signature Elements:**
- Thin gold horizontal lines as section dividers
- Ghost numerals (01–07) as large background texture elements
- Animated SVG floor plan that draws itself on scroll

**Interaction Philosophy:** Physics-based, weighted interactions. Magnetic cursor. Drag-based gallery with inertia. Horizontal scroll section for concierge services. Everything feels like it has mass.

**Animation:** GSAP + ScrollTrigger mandatory. Letter-by-letter stagger on hero. Parallax depth on all images. WebGL liquid distortion for apartment transitions. Smooth scroll with Lenis.

**Typography System:**
- Display: Tenor Sans (400) — elegant, refined, fashion-forward
- Accent/Numbers: Cinzel (400) — classical gravitas for apartment numbers
- Body: Satoshi (300, 400) — clean, modern, highly legible
- Labels: Tenor Sans, letter-spaced, uppercase
